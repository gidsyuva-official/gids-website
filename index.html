Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and programs

What is the batch size for courses? ▾
We maintain small batch sizes of 8 to 12 students to ensure personalized attention and better learning outcomes for each student.
Do you offer both online and offline classes? ▾
Yes! We offer both online live classes with recorded sessions and traditional offline classroom coaching with practical workshops and labs.
What courses do you offer? ▾
We offer NEET, JEE, CA, CMA coaching; Tally Prime with AI; Accounting & Financial Management; Computer & Business Software; Career Development Workshops; and Internship-based programs.
What professional services do you provide? ▾
We offer Income Tax Filing, GST Registration & Return Filing, Business Financial Audit, Accounting & Bookkeeping, and Compliance & Financial Advisory services.
Do you provide Tally Prime training? ▾
Yes, we provide comprehensive Tally Prime training for accountants and staff members, including installation, configuration, and business customization support.
How can I enroll for a course? ▾
You can enroll directly through our Training page by clicking the Enroll button on any course card, or call us at +91 63 74 66 94 86 for guidance.
Do you offer internship opportunities? ▾
Yes! Our Skill Development programs include project-based learning and internship opportunities with partner businesses and organizations.
What makes your platform different? ▾
We combine education, professional training, audit services, and accounting software under one roof — so you don't need to visit multiple platforms. We also maintain small batches for personal attention.
